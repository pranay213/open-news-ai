### Quick Checklist for Any Idea

1. **Validate locally** – Talk to 5‑10 potential customers before spending money.  
2. **Keep overhead low** – Use existing tools (free software, rented space, shared trailer).  
3. **Create a simple MVP** – One‑week prototype, a single pilot event, or a 2‑month trial.  
4. **Leverage word‑of‑mouth** – Ask early users for referrals or testimonials; they’re cheaper than ads.  
5. **Document the process** – A short video or photo series makes future marketing easy.

Pick the one that fits your skills, location, and the resources you already have, and start small. Success comes from consistency, not just the novelty of the idea. 🚀
