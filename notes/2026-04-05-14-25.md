### Quick Checklist to Get Started

| Step | What to Do | Tools / Resources |
|------|------------|-------------------|
| **1️⃣ Validate** | Speak to 10‑15 potential customers (e.g., local artisans for packaging, seniors for skill‑swap). | Google Forms, WhatsApp polls |
| **2️⃣ Minimal Viable Product** | Create a one‑page landing page + simple booking/scheduling system. | Carrd, Calendly, Stripe/PayPal |
| **3️⃣ Acquire First Users** | Offer the first session/experience **free** or at a 30 % discount in exchange for testimonial. | Local community groups, WhatsApp broadcasts |
| **4️⃣ Automate** | Set up email/SMS reminders, payment escrow, and feedback collection. | Zapier, Mailchimp, Twilio |
| **5️⃣ Scale** | Reinforce with partnerships (vendors, suppliers), hire part‑time assistants, expand to adjacent cities. | LinkedIn outreach, local B2B directories |

Feel free to pick the idea that resonates most with your background, resources, and community. Each one can start with a modest budget (₹ 10,000‑₹ 30,000) and grow into a sustainable side‑business or even a full‑time venture. Good luck! 🚀
