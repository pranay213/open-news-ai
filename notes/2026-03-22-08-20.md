### Quick Tips to Get Started

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **1** | Validate locally – talk to 5‑10 potential customers for each idea and note willingness to pay. |
| **2** | Build a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) – a single pop‑up workshop, one heritage‑book, one kiosk, one farmer subscription, or one VR tour. |
| **3** | Use free/low‑cost tools: Google Forms for bookings, Canva for designs, OpenAI API for text generation, Unity’s free tier for VR. |
| **4** | Leverage community groups (WhatsApp, Facebook, local NGOs) for word‑of‑mouth marketing. |
| **5** | Re‑invest the first profits into scaling – more vans, more kiosks, extra weather stations, or a small editing team. |

These five concepts are intentionally **niche, low‑entry‑barrier, and adaptable** to both urban and semi‑rural Indian contexts. Pick the one that resonates most with your skills or your locality, test it quickly, and iterate. Good luck!
