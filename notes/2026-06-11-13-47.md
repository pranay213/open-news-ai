### Quick Tips for Getting Started

| Tip | Why It Helps |
|-----|---------------|
| **Validate locally first** – Run a pilot with 5‑10 participants before scaling. | Low risk, real feedback, fast iteration. |
| **Leverage existing platforms** – Use Instagram, WhatsApp groups, or local community apps to market your service for free. | Saves marketing budget. |
| **Create a simple MVP** – A printable brochure, a Google Form for sign‑ups, or a basic website (WordPress, Carrd). | Keeps upfront costs low. |
| **Partner with complementary businesses** – e.g., grocery stores for nutrition kits, rooftop cafes for vertical‑garden demos. | Accesses an already‑interested audience. |
| **Document everything** – Capture testimonials, before‑after photos, or data points. They become powerful social proof when you expand. | Builds credibility quickly. |

Feel free to mix & match elements (e.g., combine the vertical‑garden kits with the “Skill‑Swap” lounge for a hands‑on gardening class) to tailor the ideas to your community’s needs. Good luck! 🚀
