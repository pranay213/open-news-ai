**How to get started quickly**

1. **Validate** – Talk to 3‑5 potential customers in each niche and get a “will pay” commitment.  
2. **MVP** – Build a one‑page landing page, a simple prototype (Google Form, basic Unity scene, or a low‑code app) and start charging before you perfect the product.  
3. **Leverage existing tools** – Use free/low‑cost AI APIs (OpenAI, Stability), Unity free tier, 360° cameras like Insta360 ONE X2, and marketplace platforms (Shopify for physical kits, Stripe for payments).  
4. **Iterate** – After the first 5–10 sales, gather feedback, improve the service, and then scale through referrals or micro‑ads.  

These ideas are fresh, scalable, and can be launched with modest upfront investment while tapping into growing market trends (local experiences, AI‑generated content, VR, sustainability, and data‑centric AI). Good luck!
