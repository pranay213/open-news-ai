### Quick Tips to Turn Any Idea into Cash Quickly
1. **Validate in 48 hrs** – Talk to 5–10 potential customers, get a “yes/maybe/no”. Adjust before you invest.
2. **Leverage existing platforms** – Use WhatsApp Business, Instagram, Airtable, or no‑code tools to avoid building a full website at start.
3. **Bootstrap materials** – Reuse what you already have (old phones for AR, discarded wood for furniture) to keep upfront costs low.
4. **Build a simple “proof‑of‑concept” video** – A 30‑second demo boosts credibility and makes outreach 3× faster.
5. **Collect testimonials early** – Real‑world proof lets you charge higher fees or subscription rates from week 2 onward.

Good luck! 🚀
