**How it works:**

2. Build a library of high‑performing prompts, test them, and write short usage guides.  
3. Publish the packs on a simple SaaS platform (monthly or quarterly subscription).  
4. Offer a free “starter pack” to collect email leads and upsell premium packs.  

**Revenue streams:**  
- Monthly/quarterly subscription fees.  
- One‑off premium packs for ultra‑specific needs.  
- Affiliate commissions when users upgrade to paid AI plans through your referral link.

## 2. Remote Smart‑Home Energy‑Audit & Retrofit Service  
**What it is:** A completely virtual audit of a home’s energy consumption using smart‑plug data, utility bills, and 3‑D home scans, followed by a customized retrofit plan (LEDs, smart thermostats, solar‑ready wiring, battery storage).  

**How it works:**  
1. Homeowner signs up, uploads last‑year utility bills and grants temporary access to their Wi‑Fi‑enabled smart plugs or a low‑cost plug‑in kit you ship.  
2. Your AI‑driven platform analyses usage patterns, identifies “energy vampires”, and creates a 3‑D model of the house.  
3. You deliver a PDF/interactive report with ROI calculations for each upgrade and partner‑link to purchase the recommended devices (you earn a reseller commission).  
4. Optional “implementation service” – you coordinate local certified installers and take a project‑management fee.  

**Revenue streams:**  
- $49‑$99 per audit report (one‑time).  
- 5‑10 % commission on hardware sales through your affiliate links.  
- Project‑management fee (10‑15 % of the total retrofit cost).

**How it works:**  
1. Partner with local artisans, recycling centers, and waste‑collection firms.  
2. Curate a limited‑edition “drop” each month (5‑10 pieces).  
3. Subscribers choose a style (modern, rustic, industrial) and receive one surprise item per month.  
4. Offer a “trade‑in” program – after 6 months subscribers can send the piece back for a discount on the next drop, reducing waste.  

**Revenue streams:**  
- Monthly subscription fee ($45‑$80).  
- Sale of “one‑off premium pieces” at higher price points.  
- Partnerships with interior‑design firms who buy in bulk for staging homes.

## 5. Local‑Heritage Augmented‑Reality (AR) Tour Platform  
**What it is:** An app that overlays historic photos, 3‑D reconstructions, and audio narratives onto real‑world locations via AR. The content is created in collaboration with local historians, museums, and tourism boards.  

**How it works:**  
1. Choose a heritage‑rich city/town and map 20‑30 key sites.  
2. Record short audio stories, collect archival images, and model 3‑D reconstructions of vanished structures.  
3. Publish them on a lightweight AR app (or integrate with existing AR browsers).  
4. Tourism boards pay a licensing fee to host the tour on their official websites, and you sell “premium passes” to tourists for offline use, souvenir AR‑photos, and merchandise.  

**Revenue streams:**  
- Licensing fee from municipalities/tourism boards ($2,000‑$8,000 per city).  
- Premium tourist pass ($5‑$10 per day) with offline maps, ad‑free experience.  
- In‑app purchases: AR‑photo keepsakes, themed audio guides.  
- Data‑analytics service for local businesses (foot‑traffic insights).  

---
