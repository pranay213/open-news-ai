### Quick Tips for All 5 Ideas  

| Tip | Why It Helps |
|-----|--------------|
| **Start with a pilot (1‑2 customers)** | Keeps costs low, lets you iterate before scaling. |
| **Document SOPs early** | When the model works, you can hand‑off or franchise it. |
| **Leverage community channels** (WhatsApp groups, local NGOs, neighborhood Facebook pages) | Trust is crucial for niche, hyper‑local services. |
| **Use free/low‑cost tech** – Google Forms, Stripe/Instamojo, Canva, InVideo – to set up quickly. | Reduces upfront capital and lets you focus on the product. |
| **Collect testimonials & visual proof** (photos of the rooftop garden, clips of the micro‑experience) | Social proof drives word‑of‑mouth and higher conversions. |

Feel free to pick one that resonates most with your skills, location, and resources. Good luck! 🚀
