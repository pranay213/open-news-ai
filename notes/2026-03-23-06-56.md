### Quick Tips to Launch Any of These Ideas  

1. **Validate First** – Use a short Google‑Form or Typeform poll in local Facebook/WhatsApp groups to gauge interest before building the full product.  
2. **MVP Approach** – Start with a **minimum viable product**: one skill‑snack, one pop‑up theme, one AI‑prompt template, one waste‑kit recipe, or one family’s memory vault.  
3. **Leverage Existing Tools** –  
   * **Skill‑Snacks:** Loom (video), Anchor (audio), Stripe (payments).  
   * **Pop‑Ups:** Eventbrite (tickets), Canva (promos).  
   * **AI‑Resume:** OpenAI API, Zapier (automation).  
   * **Zero‑Waste:** Dehydrator, Mason jars, QR‑code generator.  
   * **Memory‑Vault:** Notion/Notion.so + AWS S3 (storage) + GPT‑4 for narrative generation.  
4. **Community First** – Build a small loyalty group (10‑20 early adopters) who get discounts in exchange for feedback & word‑of‑mouth.  
5. **Legal & Compliance** – For any subscription or data‑storage service, ensure you have a simple **terms‑of‑service**, **privacy policy**, and comply with local tax regulations.
