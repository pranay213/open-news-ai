### Quick Tips for All 5 Ideas
1. **Validate First** – Run a tiny pilot (1‑2 weeks) before investing in inventory or tech.
2. **Leverage Community** – Use WhatsApp, Telegram, local Facebook groups, or neighborhood committees for word‑of‑mouth marketing.
3. **Keep Overheads Low** – Opt for reusable materials (e.g., fabric trays for micro‑gardens) and free/open‑source tools (Canva, Google Forms, Stripe).
4. **Add a Subscription Layer** – Even a modest recurring fee (₹200‑₹800) dramatically improves cash flow versus one‑off sales.
5. **Document & Share Results** – Customer testimonials and before‑after photos (or short clips) become powerful assets for scaling.  

Feel free to pick any of these, adapt them to your local culture, and start generating a fresh income stream!
