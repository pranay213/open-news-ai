| **3** | **Localized AR Treasure‑Hunt Tourism Platform**  <br>**Concept** – Build a mobile‑app that creates city‑specific Augmented‑Reality treasure hunts (e.g., “Historic Hyderabad Quest”). Offer **sponsorship slots** for local businesses (their logo appears as a checkpoint). <br>**Why it works** – Tourists (and locals) love interactive experiences; AR adds a tech‑novelty factor without heavy hardware. Small‑scale “escape‑room‑in‑the‑city” vibe is trending. <br>**Steps**  <br>1️⃣ Use low‑code AR SDKs (ZapWorks, Unity‑ARFoundation) – $0‑$200 to prototype. <br>2️⃣ Map 5‑7 points of interest in a city, negotiate permission from municipality or property owners. <br>3️⃣ Design riddles, QR‑code/marker placements, and a points‑system. <br>4️⃣ Launch a beta with local colleges/university groups, collect feedback. <br>5️⃣ Monetize: ticket sales, business sponsorship (₹10 k‑₹50 k per checkpoint), merchandise (t‑shirts, stickers). <br>**Potential earnings** – 150 participants × ₹500 = ₹75 k per hunt; 3 hunts/month = ₹2.25  lakh.
