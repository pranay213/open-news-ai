### Quick Tips to Turn Any Idea Into a Business

1. **Validate First** – Talk to 10‑15 potential customers before spending money.  
2. **Lean MVP** – Build the smallest version that solves the problem (e.g., a single tour, one subscription box).  
3. **Automate Payments** – Use Razorpay, Stripe, or Paytm for recurring billing.  
4. **Leverage Local Networks** – WhatsApp groups, community Facebook pages, and regional Instagram hashtags are gold for early traction.  
5. **Track Metrics** – CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost), LTV (Lifetime Value), and churn rate decide whether to scale or pivot.  

Pick the idea that resonates most with your skills, local context, and resources, and start experimenting today! 🚀
