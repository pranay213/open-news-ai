#### Quick Tips to Get Started  

| Step | What to Do |
|------|------------|
| **1️⃣ Validate Demand** | Run a 1‑minute Google Form poll in relevant Facebook/WhatsApp groups (e.g., “Would you pay ₹799 for a regional‑craft box?”). Aim for at least 30 positive responses before investing. |
| **2️⃣ Build a Minimum Viable Product** | Use free/low‑cost tools: Shopify Lite or Gumroad for subscriptions, Calendly + Zoom for AR sessions, Canva for booklet design, Instagram Reels for virtual walk teasers. |
| **3️⃣ Partner Locally** | Approach artisans, urban farms, or small‑scale equipment vendors for profit‑share agreements (e.g., 70 % to artisan, 30 % to you). |
| **4️⃣ Automate Delivery** | Set up Stripe/ Razorpay recurring billing, Zapier → Google Sheets → ShipStation (or regional courier APIs) for automatic order fulfillment. |
| **5️⃣ Collect Feedback & Iterate** | After the first month, ask customers for one‑sentence “what would make this better?” and implement the top request in the next cycle. |

These ideas blend emerging tech (AI, AR), the growing appetite for *local* & *sustainable* experiences, and subscription‑based revenue models that provide predictable cash flow. Pick any that align with your interests and start testing – the market rewards creators who move fast and listen closely! 🚀
