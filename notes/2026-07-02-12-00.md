### How to Get Started Quickly

| Step | What to Do | Tools / Resources |
|------|------------|-------------------|
| **1️⃣ Validate** | Pick one idea, talk to 5‑10 potential customers (neighbors, local shop owners, Facebook groups). Note interest & price they’d pay. | Google Forms, WhatsApp polls |
| **2️⃣ Prototype** | Build a *minimum viable product* (MVP) – a simple landing page, a mock‑up, or a single kit. | Carrd, Notion, Canva, Arduino‑kit (for sensors) |
| **3️⃣ Legal & Finance** | Register a micro‑enterprise (sole proprietorship or LLP) and open a separate bank account. | Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) portal, Paytm Payments Bank |
| **4️⃣ Launch** | Offer a *beta‑only* discount (10‑20 %). Collect feedback, iterate, then open wider. | Hootsuite for social posts, Zoom for webinars |
| **5️⃣ Scale** | Automate repetitive parts (payment, scheduling, reminders) with tools like Zapier or n8n. Add a small team (part‑time) when revenue > ₹2 Lakhs/month. | Zapier, Stripe/ Razorpay, Trello/Asana |

#### Quick Tip (Both Languages)

- **English:** Keep your first month’s **customer acquisition cost (CAC)** below 30 % of the first‑month revenue. That ensures cash‑flow positivity.
- **తెలుగు:** మొదటి నెలలో **కస్టమర్‌‑అడ్వాన్స్** (CAC) మీ ఆదాయంలో 30 % కన్నా తక్కువగా ఉండాలి. ఇలా ఉంటే, ఖర్చు‑విరుద్ధ‑లాభం త్వరగా వస్తుంది.

Happy experimenting! 🚀
