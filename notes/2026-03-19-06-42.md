### How to Get Started Quickly  
1. **Validate locally** – Run a tiny pilot (10‑20 customers) before scaling.  
2. **Leverage free tools** – Use Google Forms for pre‑registrations, Canva for branding, and Stripe/Instamojo for payments.  
3. **Build a one‑page landing page** (Carrd, Wix, or WordPress) in both English & Telugu to capture interest.  
4. **Partner early** – Approach local grocery stores, community halls, or senior clubs for cross‑promotion.  
5. **Document the process** – Even if you’re not blogging, a short case‑study video can become a powerful sales asset.

These five concepts avoid the usual freelancing or blogging routes, blend technology with community needs, and can start generating cash within weeks. Good luck!
