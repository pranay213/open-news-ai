### Quick Start Checklist (for any of the ideas)

| Step | What to Do | Approx. Cost |
|------|------------|--------------|
| **1. Validate** | Run a 1‑month survey or landing‑page test (Google Forms, Typeform) to gauge interest. | $0‑$30 |
| **2. Minimum Viable Product** | Build a simple prototype (e.g., 1‑box, 1 VR tour, 1 backyard listing). | $100‑$400 |
| **3. Legal/Compliance** | Register a business, get any needed permits (food, data‑privacy). | $50‑$150 |
| **4. Platform** | Use affordable tools: Wix/Shopify for boxes, Bubble for micro‑farm app, WordPress + WooCommerce for workshops. | $30‑$100/month |
| **5. Marketing Launch** | Targeted FB/Instagram ads, LinkedIn outreach (for corporate workshops), local community groups (for backyard farms). | $150‑$300 |
| **6. Iterate** | Collect feedback, add more niches or features, automate where possible. | Ongoing |

These ideas are designed to be **new, doable with a modest budget, and scalable** as demand grows. Pick the one that resonates most with your skills or local environment, and start testing! 🚀
