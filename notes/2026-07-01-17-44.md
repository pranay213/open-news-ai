### Quick Tips to Get Started

| Tip | Why It Helps | Practical Step |
|-----|--------------|-----------------|
| **Start Small, Test Market** | Low initial capex lets you validate demand before scaling. | Launch one pilot (e.g., 1 month of “Micro‑Repair” in a single apartment complex). |
| **Leverage Community Trust** | Word‑of‑mouth spreads faster in tight‑knit neighborhoods. | Partner with resident associations, local NGOs, or cultural clubs. |
| **Use Free/Low‑Cost Tech** | AI services (ChatGPT, DALL·E) have free tiers; they can bootstrap the creative part. | Sign up for OpenAI’s free credits, use Canva for design, Google Forms for bookings. |
| **Create Scarcity** | Limited‑edition or seat‑limited offers increase perceived value. | Publish “Only 20 slots this week” or “Only 30 boxes per design”. |
| **Add Upsell Layers** | Even a modest base price can be multiplied with add‑ons. | Offer “Premium Maintenance Pack”, “Personalized Story Video”, “One‑on‑One Vet Call”. |

Feel free to mix‑and‑match elements (e.g., combine the **Memory Capsule** narrative with the **Pet‑Tracker** app for pet‑owner souvenirs) to carve out a truly unique niche! 🚀
