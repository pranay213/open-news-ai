### How to Pick the Right One for You
| Factor | What to Ask Yourself |
|--------|----------------------|
| **Passion / Knowledge** | Do you love food, history, tech, or education? Choose the idea that aligns with your enthusiasm. |
| **Initial Capital** | Boxes & spices need inventory; VR needs a modest dev budget; garden pods need hardware. Pick the one that matches your cash flow. |
| **Local Network** | Do you have contacts with farmers, schools, or corporate HR? Leverage existing relationships to cut acquisition costs. |
| **Scalability** | Subscription‑box and AI‑study‑plan have the highest upside‑scale; garden pods and micro‑tours grow with more clients. |
| **Time to Market** | Micro‑heritage tours & spice boxes can launch in < 2 months; VR and AI services may need 3‑4 months of development. |

Pick any of the five, follow the quick‑start steps, and iterate based on early feedback. Good luck! 🚀
