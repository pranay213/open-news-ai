### Quick Tips to Get Started

| Tip | Why it matters |
|-----|----------------|
| **Test with a MVP** (Minimum Viable Product) – Build a small prototype (one pop‑up, one packaging design, one audio tour) before scaling. | Saves money & reveals real demand. |
| **Leverage Existing Platforms** – Use Shopify, WordPress, Google Forms, or QR‑code generators to avoid heavy development costs. | Keeps overhead low and speeds up launch. |
| **Partner for Credibility** – Team up with local NGOs, tourism boards, or maker‑spaces. | Gives instant trust and access to a ready audience. |
| **Collect Data Early** – Track usage, repeat purchases, or referral rates from day 1. | Data lets you tweak pricing & marketing quickly. |
| **Legal & Licensing** – For digital‑asset rentals and AR tours, have clear license agreements to protect creators and yourself. | Prevents disputes and builds professional reputation. |

Feel free to mix‑and‑match elements (e.g., combine the “Skill‑Swap” club with the “Micro‑Experience” pop‑up) to create hybrid revenue streams that feel truly original. Good luck! 🚀
