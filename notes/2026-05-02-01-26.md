### Quick‑Start Checklist for Any Idea  

1. **Validate Locally** – Talk to 5‑10 potential customers (in Telugu or English) and gauge willingness to pay.  
2. **MVP Build** – Create a minimum‑viable version that can be delivered within 4‑6 weeks.  
3. **Legal/Compliance** – Register the business, get any needed permits (e.g., venue use, solar equipment safety).  
4. **Pricing Experiment** – Test 2‑3 price points; start a little lower and raise as demand proves.  
5. **Marketing Funnel** – Use hyper‑targeted social ads (Facebook/WhatsApp groups in Telugu), local influencer shout‑outs, and a simple landing‑page with a clear CTA.

Feel free to pick the idea that resonates most with your skills and community, adapt the steps to your region, and start earning! 🚀
