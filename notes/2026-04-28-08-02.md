| **3** | **“Community Fridge Subscription Service” – Install a smart fridge in a residential complex and sell weekly “fresh‑box” subscriptions.** <br>**What it is:**  Partner with local farms or grocery wholesalers to stock a fridge with veggies, fruits, eggs, and dairy. Residents pay a **₹300‑₹600 weekly** subscription and pick up items via a QR‑code app that tracks inventory in real time. Leftover food at the end of the week is donated (CSR credit). <br>**Why it works:**  Urban families want **convenient, waste‑free grocery** options; a subscription model guarantees steady cash flow. <br>**Steps to launch:** 1️⃣ Get permission from a housing society or a coworking hub. 2️⃣ Purchase a **connected (IoT) fridge** (₹30‑40 k). 3️⃣ Sign a weekly supply contract with a farmer’s market or a wholesale dealer (pay on consignment). 4️⃣ Create a simple mobile‑web app (no‑code tools like Glide) for QR scanning & balance. 5️⃣ Market the service as “fresh food at your doorstep, no impulse buying”. |
| **4** | **“AR‑Filter Marketplace for Local Brands” – Design short Augmented‑Reality Instagram/Facebook filters for small businesses and sell them a subscription.** <br>**What it is:**  Many regional brands (clothing, tea, snack makers) want fun filters for product launches but lack in‑house designers. ### How to Choose the Best One for You

| Factor | Why it matters | Quick self‑check |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Local demand** | Ideas 1, 3 & 5 rely heavily on community size & engagement. | Do you live in a city/college town with an active local scene? |
| **Technical skill** | Idea 4 (AR filters) needs basic Spark AR knowledge; Idea 2 needs simple data‑labeling tools. | Are you comfortable learning a new software in a week? |
| **Initial capital** | Idea 3 (smart fridge) requires the biggest upfront spend; Idea 2 works with ₹5‑10 k. | How much can you invest now without jeopardizing personal finances? |
| **Time commitment** | Idea 5 can be run mostly evenings; Idea 1 may need weekend field work. | How many hours per week can you consistently allocate? |
| **Scalability** | Idea 2 and 4 can be scaled across cities quickly via remote teams. | Do you plan to stay local or build a regional business? |

Pick the one that aligns best with your strengths, budget, and the community around you, then follow the **step‑by‑step plan** provided. Good luck! 🚀
