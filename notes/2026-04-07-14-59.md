### Quick Tips to Get Started

| Step | What to Do |
|------|------------|
| **Validate** | Run a short survey (Google Forms, social media polls) with 50‑100 potential users to gauge interest and price willingness. |
| **Prototype** | Build a low‑cost MVP (e.g., a single Story‑Box or one VR tour) and test it in a local community group. |
| **Legal/Compliance** | Check zoning rules for pop‑up pods, food‑safety for snack add‑ons, and copyright for VR footage. |
| **Automation** | Use tools like **Zapier**, **Stripe**, and **Calendly** to automate bookings, payments, and reminders. |
| **Scale** | Once you have 2–3 repeat customers, replicate the model in neighboring cities or add complementary services. |

Feel free to mix & match elements (e.g., combine the **Micro‑Farm Kits** with the **Story‑Box** by featuring a local farmer’s narrative in each box). The goal is to create niche, experience‑driven products that people can’t get from generic online gigs. Good luck!
