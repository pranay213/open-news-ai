#### How to Use This List
1. **Pick the idea that resonates most with your location, skills, and budget.**  
2. **Validate quickly:** Talk to 5–10 potential customers (e.g., restaurant chefs for mushrooms, parents for snack‑boxes) and confirm willingness to pay.  
3. **Build a Minimum Viable Product (MVP)** within 30‑45 days—most of these ideas need only a modest upfront spend (₹30k‑₹80k).  
4. **Leverage free or low‑cost digital tools** (WhatsApp Business, Google Forms, free AR platforms, ChatGPT API) to keep overhead low.  
5. **Scale gradually** by adding more subscription tiers, geographic zones, or complementary services (e.g., mushroom‑infused sauces, AR tour licensing, additional rooftop sizes).

Good luck turning one of these fresh concepts into a thriving income stream! 🚀
