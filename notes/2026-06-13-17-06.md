### Quick Start Checklist for Any Idea
1. **Validate locally** – Survey 20–30 potential customers (via Google Forms, WhatsApp polls, or in‑person).  
2. **Create a Minimum Viable Product (MVP)** – One basic version (e.g., a single VR tour, one micro‑green box, one AI resume mock‑up).  
3. **Set up a simple sales funnel** – Instagram/Facebook page → landing page (Google Sites/WordPress) → payment gateway (Razorpay/Paytm).  
4. **Pilot & Collect Feedback** – Offer the MVP at a discounted “beta” price in exchange for reviews.  
5. **Iterate & Scale** – Use the feedback to improve the product, then broaden distribution (partner with local NGOs, schools, or corporate CSR programs).

Feel free to pick any of the five concepts that resonates most with your skills, resources, and community—each can start with a modest budget and grow into a sustainable side‑business or full‑time venture. Good luck!
