### Why These Ideas Are “Fresh”
| Reason | Explanation |
|--------|-------------|
| **Hyper‑local focus** | Most people think of global platforms; targeting micro‑experiences in your own neighbourhood creates low competition and high perceived value. |
| **AI infrastructure gap** | Many AI startups need cheap, high‑quality labeling. A tiny, well‑trained team can command premium rates without the tech overhead of larger firms. |
| **Cultural revival** | Subscription boxes are common, but a **single‑craft‑per‑month** narrative (and the “forgotten” angle) differentiates you from generic lifestyle boxes. |
| **IoT meets wellness** | Smart‑garden rentals blend the wellness trend with the growing demand for remote‑monitorable office perks—an underserved B2B niche. |
| **Legacy preservation** | Families increasingly want digital keepsakes; a bespoke “digital heirloom” service offers emotion‑driven value that generic photo‑apps lack. |

Feel free to adapt any of these concepts to your local market, skill set, or network. Good luck turning one of them into your next income stream! 🚀
