### Quick‑Start Checklist for Any Idea
1. **Validate the market** – run a 1‑page survey or a 2‑week pilot with 5‑10 potential customers.  
2. **Build a Minimum Viable Product (MVP)** – use cheap tools (Google Forms, Stripe, Wix, Airtable) to get the service live in < 1 month.  
3. **Set up payment & delivery logistics** – Stripe/PayPal for payments; Zapier or Make.com for automations.  
4. **Launch with a hyper‑targeted ad** – 2‑3 day Facebook/Instagram or LinkedIn campaign aimed at the exact niche (e.g., “Law firm partners”, “Mid‑size co‑working spaces”).  
5. **Iterate & scale** – collect feedback, improve the offering, then add complementary upsells (e.g., data‑analytics reports for the AI Prompt Marketplace, premium props for the adventure kits).  

Pick the one that resonates most with your skills, location, and resources, and start testing today! 🚀
