### Quick Implementation Checklist

| Idea | 1‑Week MVP | 1‑Month Test | 3‑Month Scale |
|------|-----------|--------------|----------------|
| AR‑Guided Tours | Prototype AR overlay for one local landmark (using Unity or ARCore). | Release on App Store with 2‑3 tours, promote via local tourism boards. | Add more cities, partner with travel agencies, introduce subscription model. |
| AI Meal‑Prep Kits | Simple questionnaire → Google Sheet meal plan generator. | Integrate with a local grocery API for one‑click ordering. | Expand diet options, UI redesign, negotiate bulk‑order discounts for commission. |
| Zero‑Waste Packaging | Draft a 3‑page guide + sample CAD file for a simple box. | Pitch to 3 local SMEs, produce a prototype for each. | Set up a small production line, recruit a part‑time designer, automate quoting tool. |
| Tiny Office Pods | Locate 1‑2 used vans, add basic desk & power strip. | Rent for a weekend event, gather feedback on comfort & pricing. | Purchase additional pods, add branding, offer add‑on services (coffee, printing). |
| Digital Twin Service | Capture 3D scan of your own workshop with a smartphone LiDAR app. | Offer free twin to one artisan for case‑study; charge for analytics report. | Build a SaaS portal where artisans can view & edit their twins, add subscription tiers. |

Feel free to mix and match elements (e.g., combine AR tours with the digital‑twin concept) to create even more niche opportunities. Good luck!
