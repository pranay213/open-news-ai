| **1** | **Micro‑Experience Curator for “Local‑Only” Adventures**  <br>*What it is:* Create ultra‑short, hyper‑local experiences (5‑30 min) that tourists or residents can book on a mobile app – e.g., “30‑minute rooftop sunrise coffee with a local photographer”, “5‑minute hidden‑alley street‑art walk”, “15‑minute neighbourhood‑garden tea‑time”. <br>*Why it works:* People love Instagram‑worthy moments, but most tours are hours long and expensive. | **2** | **Hyper‑Local Subscription Box – “Farm‑To‑Flat”**  <br>*What it is:* A weekly or bi‑weekly box that delivers ultra‑fresh produce (herbs, micro‑greens, edible flowers) **directly from a single urban farm or rooftop garden** to subscribers’ kitchens. ### Quick Tips to Turn Any Idea into Cash

1. **Validate Before Building** – Run a 2‑question survey (interest? price?) on a relevant Facebook/WhatsApp group.  
2. **Start Lean** – Use no‑code tools, rentals, or partnership models to keep upfront costs < 10% of the projected first‑year revenue.  
3. **Create Scarcity** – Offer the first 20 customers a “founder‑price” or a free add‑on. This accelerates word‑of‑mouth.
