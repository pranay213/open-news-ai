| Idea | 1‑Week Action | 1‑Month Milestone | 3‑Month Goal |

| Rooftop Garden | Identify 2‑3 building owners with unused roofs, present a mock‑up design. | Install a pilot garden on one roof, sign a 12‑month maintenance contract. | Scale to 5 rooftops, buy bulk hydroponic kits, generate steady monthly cash flow. |
