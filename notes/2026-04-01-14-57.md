### How to Pick & Execute

1. **Validate Quickly** – Use a 5‑question Google Form for your target audience.  
2. **MVP budget** – All five ideas can be piloted with **₹2–3 Lakhs** (hardware + minimal dev).  
3. **Revenue Timeline** –  
   *Idea 1 & 2*: Month 1‑2 (β), Month 3‑4 break‑even.  
   *Idea 3*: Month 2‑3 first event cash‑flow, Month 6 scales.  
   *Idea 4*: Month 3‑4 ROI (drone cost recovered).  
   *Idea 5*: Month 4‑5 first commission, month 8 steady flow.  
4. **Legal/Compliance** –  
   *For drones* get “Unmanned Aircraft Operator Permit” from DGCA.  
   *For music* attach a “royalty‑free” licence clause.  
   *For AR‑furniture* ensure **IP‑free 3‑D scans** (creative‑commons).  

Pick the one that matches your **skill‑set, local network, and initial capital**—and start building! 🚀
