**5 NEW Money‑Making Ideas – English + Telugu (తెలుగు)**

| 1 | **Pop‑up Solar‑Charging Pods for Events** | Purchase a few portable solar‑powered battery units, install them in a rugged case, and rent them out to festivals, weddings, sports events, or construction sites where attendees need phone/ laptop charging. | 4 | **AI‑Powered Personal Data‑Broker for Seniors** | Build a simple service that collects, organizes, and digitizes important personal documents (medical records, prescriptions, legal papers) for elderly people who may struggle with tech. AI‑సహాయంతో ఆటో‑టాగ్ చేసి, గడువు, అపాయింట్‌మెంట్‌లు లేదా రిన్యువల్స్ గురించి రిమైండర్లు పంపండి. సెటప్ ఫీజు + సురక్షిత క్లౌడ్ స్టోరేజ్ & అలర్ట్‌ల కోసం నెలవారీ చందా వసూలు చేయండి. |
| 5 | **Localized “Micro‑Tour” Subscription for Residents** | Create a hyper‑local tour service that showcases hidden gems (artisan workshops, micro‑gardens, heritage homes) in a small neighborhood or town.
