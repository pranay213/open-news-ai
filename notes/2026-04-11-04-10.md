### Quick Tips for All 5 Ideas
1. **Validate before you spend** – Talk to 5‑10 potential customers first; get their price willingness.  
2. **Start lean** – Use free or low‑cost tools (Google Forms, Stripe, no‑code app builders, free AR/VR SDKs).
