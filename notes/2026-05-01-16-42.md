### Quick “Getting‑Started” Checklist (for any of the 5 ideas)

1. **Validate locally** – Talk to 3‑5 potential customers and get a “yes” on price before you spend on tools.  
2. **Minimum Viable Product (MVP)** – Build the simplest version that solves the core problem (e.g., one mushroom shelf, one VR tour, one snack box).  
3. **Legal basics** – Register a sole‑proprietorship or LLP, and keep a basic GST/Tax compliance sheet.  
4. **Marketing hook** – Use a single‑line value proposition: “Authentic local taste in a box” / “Zero‑cost data‑privacy clean‑up” etc.  
5. **Iterate & scale** – After the first 5‑10 sales, collect feedback, adjust price or add a complementary service, and then automate (subscription, SaaS, or hiring a helper).

Choose the idea that resonates most with your skills, your local environment, and the time you can commit. All five can be started with an investment of **under ₹30,000 (≈ $380)** and have the potential to become **steady, repeat‑able income streams** within 3‑6 months. Good luck!
