#### Quick Tips to Get Started

| Step | What to Do |
|------|------------|
| **1. Validate** | Talk to 5‑10 potential customers for each idea (neighbors, local businesses, online groups). Confirm they’d actually pay. |
| **2. Minimal Viable Product (MVP)** | Build the simplest version – e.g., one rooftop micro‑farm, one AI‑prompt pack, one prototype solar kiosk, one sample experience‑box, or a 2‑month free trial of the pet‑vault. |
| **3. Legal & Logistics** | Register a micro‑LLP or proprietorship, get any required permits (e.g., rooftop usage, food handling, electric equipment), and set up a simple accounting sheet. |
| **4. Market** | Use hyper‑local Facebook groups, WhatsApp broadcasts, and community notice‑boards. Offer an “early‑bird” discount (10‑20 %). |
| **5. Iterate & Scale** | Collect feedback after the first month, tweak pricing or features, then reinvest 30 % of profits into more units, more prompts, more boxes, etc. |

Feel free to pick the one that resonates most with your skills, location, and resources—and start turning the idea into income today! 🚀
