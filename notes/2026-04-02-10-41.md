### Quick Tips for All 5 Ideas
1. **Validate early** – Use a 1‑page survey or a small pilot (5‑10 customers) before heavy investment.  
2. **Leverage existing platforms** – Instead of building everything from scratch, start with Airbnb, Stripe, Google Forms, or a no‑code site builder (Webflow, Softr).  
3. **Protect legal & compliance** – Especially for the data‑marketplace and solar‑leasing ideas; get a basic lawyer’s review or use templates from trusted startup resources.  
4. **Automation** – Set up automated emails (Zapier, Make) for onboarding, billing, and delivery to keep your time commitment low.  
5. **Scale via Partnerships** – The biggest growth lever for each concept is a strategic partner (local cafés, AI‑tool firms, heritage societies, solar installers).  

Feel free to pick any of these, tweak them to suit your local market, and start testing! 🚀
