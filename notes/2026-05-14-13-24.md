### How to Use This List

1. **Pick one idea that excites you most.**  
2. **Validate quickly:** Talk to 5‑10 potential customers (via WhatsApp groups, local meet‑ups, or online polls) and confirm they’d pay the proposed price.  
3. **Start small:** Use the “minimum viable product” (MVP) steps listed under each idea—most require ≤ ₹ 2 Lakhs initial outlay.  
4. **Iterate:** After the first month, collect feedback, adjust pricing or features, and then scale (add more locations, more themes, or premium packages).
