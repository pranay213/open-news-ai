### Quick Tips for Getting Started

| Tip | Why It Helps |
|-----|--------------|
| **Validate with a Survey** | Talk to 20‑30 potential users (office managers, heritage‑tourists, fashion‑enthusiasts) before building. |
| **Leverage Existing Platforms** | Use Gumroad, ZapWorks, or local delivery partners to minimize upfront tech cost. |
| **Start Small, Iterate** | Launch a pilot with 5‑10 users, gather feedback, then scale. |
| **Legal & Insurance** | Especially for rentals (wardrobe, solar) and food‑related kits, get basic liability coverage. |
| **Community Building** | A Facebook/WhatsApp group keeps customers engaged and turns them into brand advocates. |

Feel free to mix and match these ideas, adapt them to your city’s culture, and turn the concept into a sustainable side‑hustle or full‑time venture! 🚀
