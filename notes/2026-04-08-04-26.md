### Quick Tips for All 5 Ideas  

| Tip | Why it matters |
|-----|----------------|
| **Validate with a pilot** (1‑2 weeks, minimal spend) | Cuts risk before you invest heavily. |
| **Leverage local community groups** (WhatsApp, Facebook, neighborhood forums) | Low‑cost, high‑trust channels for early customers. |
| **Document everything** (photos of products, user testimonials, data logs) | Gives credibility for investors or future scaling. |
| **Use a simple KPI** (e.g., “orders per week” for micro‑greens, “active users per month” for AR hunts) | Keeps you focused on growth, not just ideas. |
| **Secure a small insurance or liability cover** (especially for rentals & food) | Protects you and builds trust with users. |

Feel free to pick the one that resonates most with your location, skill set, and resources — and start testing! 🚀
