### Quick‑Start Checklist (for any of the ideas)

| Step | Action | Why it matters |
|------|--------|----------------|
| **1** | Validate demand – talk to **5‑10 potential customers** (shop owners, families, apartment managers, etc.) | Confirms that people will actually pay. |
| **2** | Build a **MVP** (minimum viable product) with the cheapest tools (Google Forms, Airtable, low‑code AI, Unity free tier). | Saves money while you test. |
| **3** | Set up a **simple payment gateway** (Razorpay, Stripe) and a basic landing page. | Makes it easy to collect cash early. |
| **4** | Run a **pilot** for 2‑4 weeks, collect feedback, tweak pricing. | Refines the service before scaling. |
| **5** | Formalize the business: register, get GST, open a separate bank account. | Legitimacy and smooth tax compliance. |

Feel free to mix‑and‑match elements (e.g., combine the “Zero‑Waste Pop‑Up Kitchen” with the “Family Heritage Vault” to create nostalgic recipe boxes from old family photos). The key is to solve a micro‑pain point that big platforms have ignored, and to keep the operation simple enough that you can launch with under **₹50,000** of initial capital. Good luck!
