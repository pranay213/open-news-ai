### Quick Tips for All 5 Ideas
1. **Validate First** – Use a short survey (Google Forms) or a test run with friends to gauge demand before investing heavily.  
2. **Lean Start‑up** – Begin with a minimal viable product (MVP), collect feedback, then scale.  
3. **Legal & Tax** – Register as a sole proprietorship or LLP, keep invoices, and file GST if turnover exceeds the threshold.  
4. **Leverage Local Networks** – Community groups on WhatsApp, Facebook, and local NGOs can become your first customers and promoters.  
5. **Document & Replicate** – Create SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) for each service so you can hire helpers or launch in another city later.  

Happy innovating! 🚀
