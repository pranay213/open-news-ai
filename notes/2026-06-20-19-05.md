### Quick Tips to Get Started

| Tip | Why It Helps |
|-----|--------------|
| **Validate with a pilot** | Test the idea with 5‑10 real customers before scaling. |
| **Leverage local networks** | Word‑of‑mouth in villages, community WhatsApp groups, or regional Facebook pages cut marketing costs. |
| **Keep cost low** | Use free tools (Canva, Google Forms, Zoom) for prototypes and bookings. |
| **Document results** | Case studies → higher credibility → better pricing. |
| **Iterate monthly** | Freshness matters especially for subscription boxes and micro‑experiences. |

Feel free to mix & match elements (e.g., combine the heritage food box with a VR tour of the village where the food originates) to create even more unique revenue streams! Good luck!
