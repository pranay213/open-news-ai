### Quick Tips to Get Started

| Step | What to Do | Why it Helps |
|------|------------|--------------|
| **1️⃣ Validate demand** | Survey neighbors, local businesses, or run a one‑time free pilot. | Ensures you’re solving a real problem before investing. |
| **2️⃣ Keep the tech simple** | Use low‑code platforms (Glide, Softr) or ready‑made AR SDKs. | Cuts development time and cost. |
| **3️⃣ Build a minimal online presence** | Create a one‑page landing site (WordPress, Carrd) with clear CTA. | Captures leads and looks professional without heavy effort. |
| **4️⃣ Price by value, not time** | Focus on the outcome (e.g., “increase foot‑traffic by 15 %”) rather than hourly rate. | Customers pay more for tangible results. |
| **5️⃣ Reinforce with word‑of‑mouth** | Offer a small referral bonus (₹ 200‑₹ 500) for every new client. | Low‑cost growth engine, especially for local‑focused ideas. |

Pick the one that resonates most with your skills or the resources you already have, and start small. The fresh angle and local focus make these ideas “new” enough to stand out while still being practical to launch within a few weeks. Good luck! 🚀
