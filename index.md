### How to Choose & Get Started  
1. **Assess local demand** – walk around your city/town, check online groups, see what’s missing.  
2. **Validate with a mini‑MVP** – spend ≤ $100 to test interest (a flyer, a short landing page, or a demo video).  
3. **Secure one pilot client** before scaling – it proves the concept and gives you a testimonial.  
4. **Keep overhead low** – use existing tools (Shopify, ChatGPT API, low‑code platforms) instead of building from scratch.  
5. **Build recurring revenue** – most ideas above have a subscription or repeat‑sale component, which smooths cash flow.

Feel free to pick any of these five, tailor it to your community, and start executing! 🚀
