### Quick Tips to Launch Any of These Ideas  

1. **Validate with a Pilot** – spend 1‑2 weeks testing on a tiny scale (e.g., one pop‑up booth, one “memory‑box” order).  
2. **Leverage Free Platforms** – use WhatsApp Business, Instagram, or Google My Business for free marketing.  
3. **Keep Overheads Low** – rent only for the hours/days you need, use reusable kits, and partner with local micro‑vendors who are hungry for volume.  
4. **Collect Feedback Early** – a short Google Form or QR‑code survey helps you tweak pricing, packaging, or the experience before scaling.  
5. **Legal Basics** – register a sole‑proprietorship or LLP, get a GST registration if turnover exceeds limits, and secure any necessary local permits (e.g., pop‑up kiosk, food‑related handling).  

These concepts blend practicality with a dash of novelty, and each can be started with a modest initial investment (₹30,000‑₹80,000) while leaving room for rapid scaling once the market response is positive. Good luck! 🚀
