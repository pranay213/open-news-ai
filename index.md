### Quick Tips to Turn Any Idea into Cash

| Step | What to Do |
|------|------------|
| **Validate** | Talk to 5‑10 potential customers before spending >10 % of your budget. |
| **MVP** | Build the smallest version that delivers value (e.g., one Culture‑Box, one AR scene). |
| **Monetize Early** | Set a price and start taking payment even if the product isn’t perfect; iterate based on feedback. |
| **Leverage Partnerships** | Local artisans, restaurants, schools, or EV dealers can give you credibility and a built‑in audience. |
| **Automate** | Use Zapier, Make (Integromat) or simple scripts to convert leads → orders → delivery with minimal manual work. |
| **Scale** | Once the process is smooth, replicate in neighboring cities or add complementary products (e.g., “History‑Snack Pairings” for the AR tours). |

Feel free to pick any of these concepts, tweak it for your own region, and start testing – the first money‑making step is always *action*, not perfection! 🚀
