### Quick Checklist to Get Started

| ✔️ | Action |
|----|--------|
| 1️⃣ | Choose **one** idea that best fits your location, skills, and budget. |
| 2️⃣ | Validate demand: run a 1‑week survey or a low‑budget ad (₹1‑2 k) to gauge interest. |
| 3️⃣ | Build a **MVP** (minimum viable product) – a simple website, a prototype kit, or a demo AR hunt. |
| 4️⃣ | Acquire the first **paying customers** – offer a “founder discount” (10‑20 %). |
| 5️⃣ | Collect feedback, iterate, and then scale (more locations, more partners, or higher‑priced tiers). |

---

**Remember:** The most successful ventures start with a tiny, testable slice of the idea, earn that first ₹10 k–₹20 k, and then reinvest to grow. Choose the one that excites you the most, and dive in! 🚀
