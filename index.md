## Quick‑Start Tips for All 5 Ideas  

| Step | What to Do |
|------|------------|
| **Research the niche** | Use Google Trends, Reddit, industry forums, and local Facebook groups to confirm demand. |
| **Create a Minimal Viable Product (MVP)** | Build one sample (e.g., 3 prompt bundles, 1 micro‑tour, 1 calibration checklist, 1 DIY kit, 1 VR stage) and test with a small audience. |
| **Set up simple sales funnels** | Landing page (Mailchimp, Carrd, or Wix) → free lead magnet (e.g., “Top 5 AI Prompts” ebook) → payment gateway (Stripe, Razorpay). |
| **Collect feedback & iterate** | After the first sale, ask for a 5‑minute interview. Refine the product and pricing based on real user input. |
| **Scale with partnerships** | Partner with local businesses, eco‑brands, real‑estate agencies, or AI‑tool providers to get recurring referrals. |
| **Legal & compliance** | Register as a sole‑proprietorship/LLP, get any necessary permits (e.g., for pop‑up events), and protect intellectual property (prompt bundles, VR assets). |

---

**Feel free to mix & match** – for example, combine the DIY sustainable box with a micro‑experience pop‑up where participants make the kit together live. That synergy often creates the most buzz and faster cash flow. Good luck! 🚀
