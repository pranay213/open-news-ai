### Quick Tips to Get Started
1. **Validate locally:** Before launching, test the concept with a small group (10‑20 people) and collect feedback.  
2. **Leverage existing platforms:** Use WhatsApp groups, local Facebook pages, or community radio to spread the word without heavy ad spend.  
3. **Automate where possible:** Simple SaaS tools (Calendly for bookings, Stripe for payments, Zapier for workflow) can keep overhead low.  
4. **Document the process:** Turn the experience into case studies—this builds credibility for future scaling or franchising.  
5. **Re‑invest early profits:** Put the first month’s earnings back into marketing or better tools; growth is often exponential once the model proves solid.

Give any of these a try, adapt them to your own city or niche, and you could be on a fresh revenue stream that most people haven’t even thought about yet! 🚀
